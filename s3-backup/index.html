General

Presumed born 10 February 2014. Kody is a small-medium rescue dog, 47cm tall at the shoulders and approximately 17kg in weight. His medium-long coat is red, fawn, black, and white. His eyes are amber.

Breeds

Kody is a beautiful English Springer Spaniel mix with Pomeranian and Curly-Coated Retriever, according to a DNA report by Wisdom Panel. Which makes perfect sense: AKC describes these breeds as friendly, inquisitive, and wickedly smart.

Identification

Kody is licensed by Seattle Animal Shelter with tag number 731640. His microchip is provided by HomeAgain.





Whip Smart Working Dog

Kody is intelligent, focussed, and easy to train. He loves being in class and learning new things. After acing training and taking an advanced manners class, he moved on to complete two sections of nosework. He now spends his days running for hours in the Massachusetts countryside.





Care and Feeding

Diet, exercise, dental-, and mental-health are important. Kody eats 5 meals a day. He enjoys running, swimming, and playing at dog parks. Since moving to his rural farmhouse in 2016, he is highly active outdoors several hours nearly every day. And while he merely tolerates it, brushing his teeth 3 times a week maintains optimal dental health.





Fully Vaccinated

Kody is fully vaccinated and ready to socialize! Kody has been vaccinated continuously since 5 months old, the approximate age at which he was rescued.
